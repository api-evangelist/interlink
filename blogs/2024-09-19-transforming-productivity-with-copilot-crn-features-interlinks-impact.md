Interlink Cloud Advisors, a top Microsoft partner and early Copilot adopter, is making waves in the AI and Copilot space; with Rachael Narel, Employee Engagement and Productivity Practice Lead, leading the charge. As highlighted in the recent CRN article, “Satya Nadella Has Microsoft in the AI Driver’s Seat” , Narel's Microsoft-funded workshops are drawing a diverse group of attendees from a wide range of industries, from CEOs to IT professionals, showcasing the wide-reaching potential of AI and Copilot in almost any workspace. This engagement from top executives is not only driving interest but also fostering a culture of involvement among end users. According to Narel, “Seeing that the executives are engaged on this makes the end users feel more engaged in this as well,”.
